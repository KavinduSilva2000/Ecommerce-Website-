About Us

We Bandoo Fashion (Private) Limited is the one of the leading companies in the fashion retail trade with exclusive designer wear inventory which increases daily, ranging from the latest fashions to the most modern accessories and everything in the one-stop shop for all your shopaholic needs. And its main intention is to introduce the current fashion trends in the international market to Sri Lanka which suits Sri Lankans as well as any fashion followers.Bandoo Fashion (Private) Limited entered the fashion retail industry in 20i8 with one outlet at Malabe. In 2020 RV kept its 2nd biggest footstep by opening an outlet at Battaramulla and in 2021 RV was able to fulfill the fashion desires of Kaduwela area customers. Bandoo Fashion kept its biggest step in 2023 by opening the cooperative office at koswatta.
